## The setup

A VPS running Coolify, which handles the boring parts of hosting: Docker
builds, reverse proxy, TLS, and push-to-deploy. It's where this site lives,
alongside pyxyll.com and anything else of mine that needs a backend.

The point was to stop renting a separate platform for every little thing and
just keep one box I actually understand.
